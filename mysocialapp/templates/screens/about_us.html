About Us

Welcome to Shine In Darkness

Our Vision

At Shine In Darkness, we envision a world where individuals living with Stargardt disease can thrive, supported by a compassionate and understanding community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging and empowerment among individuals affected by Stargardt disease.

  • Provide a safe and inclusive environment for open discussions and information sharing.
  • Offer resources and support to help individuals better understand and manage their condition.
  • Facilitate connections and relationships among community members, fostering a sense of solidarity and mutual support.
  • Raise awareness about Stargardt disease and advocate for increased understanding and accessibility.

What We Offer

  • Community Forums: Engage in discussions, ask questions, and share experiences with others in the Stargardt community.
  • News and Research: Stay updated with the latest news and research developments related to Stargardt disease.
  • Volunteer Assistance: Connect with volunteers offering support and assistance tailored to your needs.
  • Accessibility Features: Our platform is designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring that individuals with visual impairments can fully participate and engage.

Our Commitment

At Shine In Darkness, we are committed to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all individuals affected by Stargardt disease.

We adhere to principles of inclusivity, respect, and confidentiality, and we strive to create a space where everyone feels valued and heard.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to support individuals living with Stargardt disease.

Whether you're looking to connect with others, share your story, or contribute to our community, we welcome your participation and involvement.
